🏛️ Glory of Rome — Complete Guide

📋 Overview

  • Glory of Rome is a turn-based tactical battle game featuring famous historical battles and random scenarios.
  • Play as legendary commanders: Caesar, Scipio, Augustus, Antony, and Sulla.
  • Victory is achieved by eliminating all enemy units or destroying their general.

🎮 Game Modes

  • Historical Battles: Pre-deployed armies in famous engagements. Learn tactics from history!
  • Random Battle: Traditional deployment mode where both sides place units in their first three rows.

📜 Historical Scenarios

  • Battle of Cannae (216 BC) - ★★★ Hardest: Play as Rome facing Hannibal's legendary double envelopment. You must overcome his superior tactics.
  • Battle of Teutoburg Forest (9 AD) - ★★★ Hardest: Dense forests hide Germanic warriors. Recover from this historical disaster.
  • Battle of Phalaphiris (48 BC) - ★★ Moderate: Play as Caesar. Balanced forces with diverse unit types.
  • Battle of Philippi (42 BC) - ★★ Moderate: Play as Augustus & Antony. Coordinate two armies to avenge Caesar.
  • Battle of the Nile (47 BC) - ★ Easy (New Players): Play as Caesar in Egypt. Perfect for learning.
  • Battle of Chaeronea (86 BC) - ★ Easy (New Players): Play as Sulla. Introductory difficulty.
  • Battle of Zama (202 BC) - ★★ Moderate: Scipio's triumph over Hannibal. The culmination of the Punic Wars.
  • Random Battle - Varies: Symmetric deployment. You deploy on rows 7-9, enemy on rows 0-2.

🎮 Game Flow

  • Random Battle Deployment Phase: Place your units and structures (towers/walls) on your side of the battlefield (rows 7-9).
  • Historical Battle Start: All units are pre-deployed. Combat begins immediately.
  • Combat Phase: Take turns moving and attacking units. After you act, the AI moves 2-4 of their units, then control returns to you.
  • Each turn: You can move and act with as many units as stamina allows. Press "Next Turn" when done to end your turn.

⚔️ Combat Mechanics

  • Unit Selection: Click a unit to select it. Available moves/attacks are highlighted in yellow.
  • Movement: Click a highlighted tile to move. Each movement costs stamina based on terrain.
  • Attacks: Melee units attack adjacent tiles (except Triarii, who can attack 2 tiles away). Ranged units attack 1-3 tiles away (4 from towers).
  • Damage Calculation: Attacker's attack value × multipliers - defender's defense reduction = damage.
  • General Aura: Units within 3×3 area around a General get 1.2× damage boost!
  • Cavalry Restriction: Cavalry units can ONLY attack once per turn (but can move multiple times with half stamina cost).

❤️ Healing System (NEW!)

  • Heal Button: When you select a unit, a ❤️ Heal button appears at the bottom.
  • Cost: 6 stamina converts to 1/8 (12.5%) HP recovery.
  • Example: A unit with max 4 HP heals 0.5 HP per heal action.
  • Insufficient Stamina: If a unit has less than 6 stamina, healing does nothing.
  • Strategic Use: Heal key units between turns or late in battle to preserve forces.

⚡ Stamina System (Critical!)

  • All units start with 8 stamina (Generals get 12).
  • Each turn, stamina recovers by 4 (Cavalry recover 2 extra due to half-cost efficiency).
  • Movement Costs:
    • Plains: 1 stamina
    • Trees: 2 stamina
    • Mountain Level 1: 3 stamina
    • Mountain Level 2+: 4 stamina
    • Water: 8 stamina (very expensive!)
  • Attack Costs:
    • Melee Attack: 4 stamina
    • Ranged Attack: 3 stamina
    • Healing: 6 stamina (→ 1/8 HP recovered)
  • Cavalry (including Generals) use HALF stamina costs for movement and attacks.
  • Cavalry can ONLY attack ONCE per turn to prevent overpoweredness.
  • Red Stamina Bar: Shows remaining stamina. When empty, the unit cannot move or attack until next turn.

👥 Unit Types

  • Legionary (LEG): Basic infantry. Balanced stats. 3 HP, 1 ATK, 3 Move.
  • Hastati (HAST): Light infantry. Same as Legionary. 3 HP, 1 ATK, 3 Move.
  • Princeps (PRIN): Heavier infantry. 4 HP, 1 ATK, 3 Move.
  • Triarii (TRIA): Elite infantry. Strongest melee. Can attack 2 tiles away. 4 HP, 2 ATK, 3 Move.
  • Velites (VEL): Ranged archer. 2 HP, 2 ATK, 3 Move, 2 Range.
  • Sagittarii (SAG): Ranged archer. 2 HP, 2 ATK, 3 Move, 2 Range.
  • Equites (CAL): Cavalry. Fast and strong. Uses half stamina. Can attack ONLY ONCE per turn. 3 HP, 2 ATK, 5 Move.
  • General (GEN): Rare elite unit. 1.5× HP, ATK, Stamina compared to cavalry. Can attack ONLY ONCE per turn. **Killing a General wins the battle instantly!** Displayed as a gold star. 4.5 HP, 3 ATK, 5 Move, 12 Max Stamina. Nearby units gain 1.2× damage!
  • Carthage Infantry (CART INF): Carthaginian heavy infantry. Used by Carthage in Cannae/Zama.
  • Carthage Cavalry (CART CAV): Carthaginian cavalry. Used by Carthage in Cannae/Zama. Half stamina for actions like Roman cavalry.
  • Elephant (EL): Carthaginian war elephant, massive HP/damage, slow but deadly. Blockbuster in Cannae/Zama.
  • Warrior (WAR): Barbarian infantry. Only in random/Teutoburg.
  • Archer (ARCH): Barbarian archer. Only in random/Teutoburg.

���️ Structures

  • Tower: Archers can garrison towers. Towers reveal enemies in trees within a 5×5 area and grant archers ×3 damage multiplier. Towers have a shield that absorbs the first hit.
  • Wall: Infantry (non-archers, non-cavalry) can occupy walls. Units on walls gain 2× defense (damage halved) and 1.5× attack power.

🌍 Terrain & Visibility

  • Plains: Open ground. No penalties. Movement cost = 1.
  • Trees: Dense forest. AI units in trees are invisible unless you're adjacent or have a tower nearby. Your units in trees show a 👁️ icon. Movement cost = 2.
  • Mountains: High terrain. Uphill charges deal more damage (1.5× for most units, 3× for cavalry). Higher elevation = higher movement cost (3 or 4 stamina).
  • Water/Lakes: Very difficult terrain. Cannot be crossed normally but can be forced at huge stamina cost (8 stamina per tile).
  • Tower Reveal: A tower reveals any unit in a 5×5 area around it (Chebyshev distance ≤ 2), even if hiding in trees.

✨ General Aura

  • General Buff: All friendly units within a 3×3 area around a General deal 1.2× damage.
  • Strategic Positioning: Keep your General near key units to amplify their effectiveness.
  • Risk: Protecting your General is critical—if killed, you lose immediately!

📊 Battle Status & Sidebar

  • Sidebar (right panel): Shows all current units, their stats, stamina levels, structures, and battle information.
  • Status Bar (top): Displays whose turn it is and current game phase.
  • Unit Info: When you select a unit, shows its stats (HP, ATK, Move Range). A healing button also appears!

🎯 Random Deployment Strategy Tips

  • You deploy on rows 7-9 (bottom). AI deploys on rows 0-2 (top).
  • Place ranged units (archers) on towers for protection and damage boost.
  • Place melee units on walls for defense advantage.
  • Spread cavalry across your formation for quick flanking maneuvers (but remember: ONE attack per turn!).
  • Protect your general—if they're exposed and killed, you lose instantly!

⚙️ Combat Strategy Tips

  • Stamina Management: Don't waste stamina on mountain crossings unless necessary. Plan efficient routes.
  • Cavalry Efficiency: Use cavalry to scout and harass with ONE focused attack per turn because they only use half stamina.
  • Cavalry Positioning: Position cavalry for maximum impact with that single attack—flank weak units or finish wounded enemies.
  • Ranged Support: Keep archers on towers or walls to maximize damage while staying safe.
  • Uphill Charges: Attack downhill enemies for damage bonus (charge from higher ground to lower).
  • Tree Advantages: Hide units in trees before attacking. AI can't see you unless they're adjacent.
  • General Hunt: If you spot the enemy General, focus fire to eliminate them and win instantly!
  • General Aura: Position your General to support key attackers with 1.2× damage multiplier.
  • Healing: Use the ❤️ Heal button to recover units strategically. 6 stamina = 1/8 HP is efficient for mid-battle recovery.

🎮 Controls During Battle

  • Click a unit: Select it (white border appears). Healing button appears if player-controlled.
  • Click highlighted tile: Move to that tile or attack.
  • Click another unit: Switch selection.
  • Heal Button (❤️): Click to heal selected unit (6 stamina → 1/8 HP). Only works if you have enough stamina.
  • Next Turn button: End your actions. AI will then move 2-4 units.
  • Skip Animation button: Skip the AI animation and move immediately to next turn.

🏆 Winning & Losing

  • You Win If:
    • You eliminate all enemy units, OR
    • You kill the enemy General (instant victory!)
  • You Lose If:
    • All your units are eliminated, OR
    • Your General is killed (instant defeat!)

📝 Unit Bar Explanations

  • Top Bar (Green): HP (health points). When it reaches 0, the unit dies.
  • Bottom Bar (Red): Stamina. When depleted, the unit cannot act until next turn.
